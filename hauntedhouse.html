Night at the Haunted Museum

Technical Director
March 2022 - October 2022

Project Overview



100% Complete

Project Goal:

Design build an operate Northwestern's very first haunted house.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Lead a team of more than 30 designers
  • Ideate technical elements to contribute to guest experience
  • Ensure feasibility and cohesion of designs
  • Map storyline and design elements to physical space through floor and elevation plans
  • Organize team meetings, timelines, bill of materials, and purchasing trips

Key Results:

  • Fundraised more than 12000 dollars for the project from various funding authorities
  • Negotiated contracts for pipe and drape, venue, and sound equipment rental
  • Operated for 3 hours, entertaining more than 550 guests [with a theoretical hourly capacity of 1440]
  • Maintained both scare actor and guest safety, with successful execution of emergency protocols
